ETERNA Extra Long Cleaning Gloves

Built for tough cleaning jobs, these gloves offer unmatched durability and protection to keep your hands safe and comfortable all day long.

Durable & Comfortable Design

With an extra-long sleeve and expandable construction, these gloves provide a secure, customizable fit that adapts to different tasks and arm sizes. Whether you're tackling heavy-duty cleaning in the home or working on your car, the extended length ensures full coverage and protection without sacrificing flexibility.

Enhanced Cleaning Efficiency

The unique brush texture on the fingertips is designed to tackle stubborn stains and delicate fabrics with ease. This feature makes them an excellent choice for both laundry and household cleaning, ensuring you can work efficiently without damaging your tools or materials.

Superior Grip & Safety

A diamond-patterned palm provides a firm grip on cleaning tools, reducing the risk of slips and enhancing overall safety. This makes them ideal for tasks that require precision and stability, such as scrubbing countertops or cleaning tight spaces.

The chlorinated material not only gives the gloves a smooth, comfortable feel but also resists bacterial growth, keeping your hands clean and safe. Plus, the built-in water channels prevent moisture from entering the interior, ensuring your hands stay dry during extended cleaning sessions.

Whether you're a DIY enthusiast, a busy parent, or a professional cleaner, these ETERNA gloves are designed to meet the needs of every task. They combine functionality, comfort, and safety to make cleaning a smoother, more enjoyable experience.
