Venom Steel Industrial Nitrile Gloves

Built for durability and reliable protection, Venom Steel nitrile gloves are the perfect choice for professionals working in demanding environments. Their unique two-layer construction ensures superior defense against chemical exposure, tears, punctures, and abrasions, making them a trusted partner for safety on the job.

Featuring a 6 mil thickness, these gloves are 70% thicker than standard disposable options, providing enhanced strength and long-lasting wear. The black outer layer combines with a white inner layer to clearly show if a chemical has breached the glove, adding peace of mind with every task. Additionally, they're powder-free and free from natural rubber latex, making them an ideal fit for those with sensitive skin or allergies.

Designed for heavy-duty use, Venom Steel gloves are essential for industries like automotive, marine, construction, and more. Their textured grip offers excellent traction and control, allowing for a secure hold on tools and equipment. Enjoy all-day comfort without sacrificing protection when handling oils, fuels, and greasy substances.

Available in a pack of 500, these gloves are not only ideal for daily use but also perfect for bulk storage, ensuring you're always prepared for your next challenge.
